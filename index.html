That’s right! Even taking into consideration the fact that technology changes damn rapidly nowadays. It’s even not so easy to keep abreast of the news about the bleeding edge stuff. But there’s a way, the way of learning from others and sharing what you’ve been learned and this way called the unconference.

We selected the format which is close to the original unconference format, because it creates the space for peer-to-peer learning, collaboration and creativity. Anybody can submit the talk, and the agenda is created by the attendees themselves. The sessions might be formal or informal, full of years of experience or just an introductory talk to something new.
