Why it is so different?

  • Community-driven event

    It's community driven conference, so we know what guys and gals like us need. It's a social event — the rare kind of IT events, which are mostly technology or vendor specific.

  • Picturesque place

    As a resort city of all seasons, Jurmala offers a wide range of leisure activities and our venue the Baltic Beach Hotel is the most elegant SPA hotel in Jurmala.

  • “Everyone's invited!”

    That's the motto of this conference. Why? The reason is to show that no matter what part of IT we're involved, we can learn from each other and nurture our knowledge and experience together.

Organizers

Latvian Developers Network

It's all about not being locked in a certain platform or company's culture. LDN is a possibility to share own thoughts, experience and passion for software creation among the whole community.

Agile Latvia

Agile Latvia is a community with a goal to spread information about Agile practices amongst Latvian software development professionals and industry newcomers.

Latvian Oracle User Group

LVOUG is the collective “voice” for Oracle users in Latvia. It serves to protect the interests of the Latvian Oracle community and provides an essential source of information for all Oracle professionals.
